Seccomp-Diff

Built for Shmoocon 2025

Seccomp-Diff is a tool designed to compare seccomp filters applied to containers, addressing the challenges of identifying and maintaining secure configurations. By highlighting differences between two containers, it helps ensure more secure setups compared to runtime defaults.

Useage:List the containers and select two containers you'd like to compare. Click the Seccomp Diff button to compare what the kernel sees the applied bpf filters are.
